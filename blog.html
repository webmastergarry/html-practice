The Importance of Cybersecurity

The importance of cybersecurity cannot be understated. Everyday, we face attacks as individuals, groups and nations by people that want to steal, destroy or control.
As cybersecurity students and experts, it is our job to protect not only ourselves, others or our country, but the safety of humanity as a whole.

You might think to yourself: "What can I do? What would make me any good in cybersecurity? Isn't hacking only for geniuses?"
There is only one truth - everyone can take a part. While we may not all be the next Kevin Mitnick, Edward Snowden or even Anonymous, we all have a part to play, no matter how little it may be. Everyone can learn basic cybersecurity - the area you reach is entirely dependent on your passion and persistance. It requires coding until your fingers go numb, it requires throwing yourself into risky territory and it requires a will to do good.
I believe it is everyone's best interest and duty to protect those we love - we are solgiers, we are warriors and we are fighters.
We are the sword and shield in the era of computers - and we must fight for what's right.

It is our duty. It is our will. It is our passion.
We will not stop, we will not give up and we will not surrender.
And it begins, one script at a time.
